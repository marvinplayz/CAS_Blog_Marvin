What is CAS?

CAS (Creativity, Activity, Service) is a core component of the IB Diploma that encourages students to engage in creative pursuits, physical activities, and community service to develop personal and interpersonal skills beyond academics. It requires students to set goals, reflect on their experiences, and demonstrate growth in various aspects of life.
